Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

From the article progressive enchancement is a way of building websites so that everyone can use them, no matter what browser or device they have. It starts with the basic stuff first, like text and links, and then adds extra features for people with newer browsers or phones and tablets. This makes websites easier for everyone to access.
