Welcome to the Red Planet: Exploring Mars with NASA's Rovers

For decades NASA has been exploring the mysteries of Mars, sending robotic rovers across its rockey terrain in search of answers to some of humanitys biggest questions: Is there life beyond Earth? What can Mars teach us about our own planet?

From the groundbreaking pathfinder mission in 1997 to the cutting-edge rovers that have been traversing Mars and making amazing discoveries for the human exploration.
