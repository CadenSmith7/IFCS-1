Frequently Asked Questions

How many rovers has NASA sent to Mars?

NASA has sent several rovers to Mars, including Sojourner, Spirit, Opportunity, Curiosity, and Perseverance.

Which rover is still active?

As of 2025, the Curiosity and Perseverance rovers are still actively exploring Mars.

How do the rovers land on Mars?

NASA has used a variety of landing systems, including airbags and sky cranes. Curiosity and Perseverance both used a sky crane system.

What is the goal of the Mars rover missions?

Each rover is designed to explore Mars' surface, study its geology, and search for signs of past or present life.
